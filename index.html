  • Why This Matters >

    • Many people struggle with budgeting, saving, and planning for the future, which often leads to debt, missed opportunities, or financial stress.

    Head$tart simulates these financial decisions so you can understand how choices you make every day affect your financial health over time. By playing, you’ll gain insight into:

    1. How to balance income, expenses, and savings.
    2. The impact of emergencies or opportunities on your financial stability.
    3. The importance of planning ahead and making informed decisions.
  • How to Play >

    • At the top of the screen, you'll always see your current age, income, and net worth. These numbers change in real-time as you make decisions. Your age will automatically increase as you progress through the game, while your income and net worth react to the choices you make.

    When faced with a decision, you'll see options presented in green panels on the left side of the screen. To choose an option, type the corresponding letter (like "a" or "b") or number (like "1" or "2") and either press Enter on your keyboard or click the Submit button.

    After each decision, the right panel will show you the consequences of your choice. This is where you'll get personalized feedback about your decisions and a narrative formed for your story.

    The game continues until you reach retirement age. Your goal is to become financially aware and work towards building a high net worth while also time for experiences.

  • Progress & Income >
    • The progress bar tracks your age as you advance toward retirement, and your income is based on your current job or education path. Everyone starts with $1,000 net worth, but make sure to keep a watch on it to see how it changes!
  • Life Events >
    • Expect bills, emergencies, and opportunities. Your choices impact your net worth and game outcome.
  • Strategy Tips >
    • Save early, avoid debt, and invest wisely to grow your net worth over time - but don’t forget to enjoy life along the way. Balance is key!
