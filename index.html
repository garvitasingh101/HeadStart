Welcome to Head $tart!

Learn from making decisions and create your financial story!

Head $tart was created to educate high school students about financial decisions they’ll face in real life. Through engaging scenarios and choices, users learn how their decisions impact their income, savings, and overall financial health.

  • Why This Matters >

    • Many people struggle with budgeting, saving, and planning for the future, which often leads to debt, missed opportunities, or financial stress.

    Head$tart simulates these financial decisions so you can understand how choices you make every day affect your financial health over time. By playing, you’ll gain insight into:

    1. How to balance income, expenses, and savings.
    2. The impact of emergencies or opportunities on your financial stability.
    3. The importance of planning ahead and making informed decisions.
  • How to Play >
    • Manage your money by making smart choices on spending, saving, and investing while navigating life events. Watch your budget and progress bar to track your financial health.
  • Budget & Progress >
    • Your budget shows your current money. The progress bar tracks your journey and financial stability.
  • Life Events >
    • Expect bills, emergencies, and opportunities. Your choices impact your net worth and game outcome.
  • Strategy Tips >
    • Save early, avoid debt, and think long-term to build wealth and win the game.
