  • Why This Matters

    • Many people struggle with budgeting, saving, and planning for the future, which often leads to debt, missed opportunities, or financial stress.

    Head$tart simulates these financial decisions so you can understand how choices you make every day affect your financial health over time. By playing, you’ll gain insight into:

    1. How to balance income, expenses, and savings.
    2. The impact of emergencies or opportunities on your financial stability.
    3. The importance of planning ahead and making informed decisions.
  • How to Play

    • At the top of the screen, you'll see your age, income, and net worth. These update as you progress.

    When making choices, type the letter (like "a") or number (like "1") shown next to an option, then press Enter or click Submit.

    The right panel displays how your choice affected your character and finances.

    Your goal is to build strong financial habits and a high net worth before retirement!

  • Progress & Income
    • The progress bar shows your aging timeline. Your income and net worth update based on job paths and decisions.
  • Life Events
    • Expect bills, emergencies, and opportunities. Your decisions shape your financial outcome.
  • Strategy Tips
    • Save early, avoid debt, invest smart, and enjoy life — balance is key!
